New Features in Version 1.3

Preprocessor:

• Gravity drain boundary condition.
• Rossi-Nimmo hydraulic characteristic functions.
• No limitation on the number of columns and rows in the grid.
• No limitation on the number of textural classes, "possible seepage face" boundary segments, iterations per time step, observation points, evapotranspiration periods, and output times.
• Source or sink points may be specified in the model domain.
• The x-z (or x-r) coordinates at a vertex of a domain boundary, textural zone, or contour may be explicitly specified by right-clicking on the vertex.
• Multiple, consecutive recharge periods can be added, edited, or deleted in the Recharge Period Window.
• Boundary condition at a domain boundary segment may be specified by double clicking the segment.
• Boundary condition data may be imported from file.
• Evapotranspiration period data may be imported from file.
• Comments are added to the model input file generated for VS2DH 3.3.

Postprocessor:

• The boundary between textural zones may be displayed.
• Incorporates version 3.3 of VS2DH.
• The fluid and energy balance calculation has been revised to correctly account for instantaneous changes in fluid and energy within the simulated domain due to instantaneous changes in boundary conditions. For more information, see the section on "Fluid and Energy Balance".
