Tutorial: Energy Balance Options

In the energy balance panel, mass balance components for energy are selected to be written to auxiliary model output file "balance.out". For this example 4 selections are made: Total Energy Movement In and Out of the Domain, Change in Energy Storage in the Domain, and Energy Balance.

Filling in the energy balance panel completes entries of "Model Options". You should now click the "OK" button. The next step in the tutorial is to view the textural class data.
