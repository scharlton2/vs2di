Deleting Observation Points

Before you begin, make sure that "Observation Points" is selected in the "Active Data" Selector (see ).

1. Click the  button.

2. Move the cursor to the observation point and press the left mouse button. The observation point will become highlighted. To select multiple points, hold down the "Ctrl" key and successively click on each point. To select all points, click the "Edit" menu and select "Select All".

3. Click the "Edit" menu and select "Delete" or press the "Del" key.

4. Deletions may be undone by selecting "Undo" from the "Edit" menu.
