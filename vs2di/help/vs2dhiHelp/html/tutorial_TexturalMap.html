Tutorial: Textural Map

To view and/or modify the model textural map, select "Textural Map" from the "Active Data" Selector (see ).

Two polygonal zones are displayed, one for each of the active textural classes. The example domain consists primarily of sand. There is a small sloping silt lens in the middle of the domain.

You may:
 •  Draw a textural zone
 •  Reshape a textural zone
 •  Move textural zones
 •  Insert a vertex on textural zone
 •  Bring textural zones to front
 •  Send textural zones to back
 •  Delete textural zones
