Drawing the Chemical Map

The chemical map shows the spatial distribution of chemical class (chemical properties). The chemical map is composed of "chemical zones" each having one or more uniform properties. Each zone is represented by a polygon (see ).

To draw the chemical map, select "Chemical Map" from the "Active Data" Selector (see ).

You may then:
 •  Draw a chemical zone
 •  Reshape a chemical zone
 •  Move chemical zones
 •  Insert a vertex on chemical zone
 •  Bring chemical zones to front
 •  Send chemical zones to back
 •  Delete chemical zones

Buttons on the left panel allow display of coverages of each of the 7 chemical properties. Clicking on a button will show which chemical classes are used to define that property.

Notes:

• Chemical zones may overlap one another. Definitions of property values in those portions of chemical zones that are covered by other chemical zones are determined on the basis of options selected when you specify chemical classes.
• Chemical zones may extend outside the domain. (They will be "trimmed" by the program.)
• All portions of the domain must be covered by a chemical zone
