Inserting a Vertex On a Chemical Zone

Before you begin, make sure that "Chemical Map" is selected in the "Active Data" Selector (see ).

1. Click the button.

2. Place the cursor on the edge of a chemical zone where the new vertex is to be inserted. (Avoid placing the cursor on an existing vertex.)

3. Click the left mouse button

4. Repeat steps 2 - 3 to insert additional vertices.
