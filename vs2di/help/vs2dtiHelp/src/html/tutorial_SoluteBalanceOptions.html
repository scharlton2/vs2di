Tutorial: Solute Balance Options

In the solute balance panel, mass balance components for solute are selected to be written to auxiliary output file "balance.out". For this example 4 selections are made: Total Solute Movement In and Out of the Domain, Change in Solute Storage in the Domain, and Solute Balance.

Filling in the solute balance panel completes entries of "Model Options". You should now click the "OK" button. The next step in the tutorial is to view the textural class data.
