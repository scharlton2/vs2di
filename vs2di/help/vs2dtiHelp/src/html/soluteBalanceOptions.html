Energy/Solute Balance Options

Select any combination of the following energy/solute balance components to be written to the auxiliary output file "balance.out". For each selected component, 3 entries are written for energy and each solute species: total for simulation, total for time step, and rate for time step.

Specified head, Advection In: Advective transport into domain across specified pressure and total head boundaries

Specified head, Advection Out: Advective transport out of domain across specified pressure and total head boundaries and seepage faces

Specified Flux, Advection In: Advective transport into domain across specified fluid flux boundaries

Specified Flux, Advection Out: Advective transport out of domain across specified fluid flux boundaries

Dispersion/Conduction, In: Dispersive and diffusive solute transport and conductive energy transport into domain across all boundaries

Dispersion/Conduction, Out: Dispersive and diffusive solute transport and conductive energy transport out of domain cross all boundaries

Evapotranspiration: Energy/solute transport out of domain by transpiration (plant root extraction) and energy loss from bare soil evaporation

Total Energy/Solute, In: Total energy/solute into domain by transport and reactions

Total Energy/Solute, Out: Total energy/solute out of domain by transport and reactions

Change in Energy/Solute Storage: Change in energy/solute mass stored in domain

Energy/Solute Balance: Total in - total out - change in storage
