Solute Balance Options

Select any combination of the following solute balance components to be written to the auxiliary output file "balance.out". For each selected component, 3 entries are written: total for simulation, total for time step, and rate for time step.

Specified head, Advection In: Advective solute transport into domain across specified pressure and total head boundaries

Specified head, Advection Out: Advective solute transport out of domain across specified pressure and total head boundaries and seepage faces

Specified Flux, Advection In: Advective solute transport into domain across specified fluid flux boundaries

Specified Flux, Advection Out: Advective solute transport out of domain across specified fluid flux boundaries

Dispersion/Diffusion, In: Dispersive and diffusive solute transport into domain across all boundaries

Dispersion/Diffusion, Out: Dispersive and diffusive solute transport out of domain cross all boundaries

Total Solute, In: Total solute into domain by transport and reactions

Total Solute, Out: Total solute out of domain by transport and reactions

Decay: Solute loss by decay

Adsorption or Ion Exchange: Solute loss by adsorption or ion exchange

Transpiration: Solute transport out of domain by transpiration (plant root extraction)

Change in Solute Storage: Change in solute mass stored in domain

Solute Balance: Total solute in - total solute out - change in solute mass storage
