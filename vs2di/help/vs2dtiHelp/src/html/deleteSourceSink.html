Deleting Source/Sink Point

Before you begin, make sure that "Source/Sink Points" is selected in the "Active Data" Selector (see ).

1. Click the  button.

2. Move the cursor to the source/sink point and press the left mouse button. The source/sink point will become highlighted. To select multiple points, hold down the "Ctrl" key and successively click on each point. To select all points, click the "Edit" menu and select "Select All".

3. Click the "Edit" menu and select "Delete" or press the "Del" key.

4. Deletions may be undone by selecting "Undo" from the "Edit" menu.
