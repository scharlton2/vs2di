Heat transport properties

• Long. disp. (alpha-L): [m] Longitudinal dispersivity. NOTE, heat and solute dipersivities should be given identical values.
• Trans. disp. (alpha-T): [m} Transverse dispersivity.
• Cs: Heat capacity of dry solids, [J/m3 oC].
• KTr: Thermal conductivity of water-sediment at residual moisture content, [W/moC].
• KTs: Thermal conductivity of water-sediment at full saturation, [W/moC].
• Cw: Heat capacity of water, which is the product of density times specific heat of water, [J/m3 oC].

Solute transport properties

• Long. disp. (alpha-L): [L] Longitudinal dispersivity.NOTE, heat and solute dispersivities should be given identical values.
• Trans. disp. (alpha-T): [L} Transverse dispersivity.
• Coef. mol. diff.: [L2]/[T]. Coefficient of molecular diffusion in the porous medium.
