Editing an Evapotranspiration Period

If the Evapotranspiration Window is not visible, display it by clicking "Show" and then selecting "Evapotranspiration Window".

In the Evapotranspiration Window:

1. Click (select) the row containing the evapotranspiration period to be edited.

2. Click "Edit". The "Edit Evapotranspiration Period" dialog box is displayed.

3. Change the evaporation parameters and/or the transpiration parameters.

4. Click "OK".
