Drawing Interior Boundaries

Note: Skip this step if you do not have to exclude any interior region from the domain.

Before you begin, make sure that "Domain" is selected in the "Active Data" Selector (see ).

1. Click the button.

2. Draw the interior boundary (polygon) by clicking on its vertices. (Click with left mouse button.)

3. Double click the last vertex.

4. Repeat steps 2 - 3 to draw additional interior boundaries.
