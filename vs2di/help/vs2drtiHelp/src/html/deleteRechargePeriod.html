Deleting One or More Recharge Periods

Note: Deleting a recharge period will also delete the boundary conditions associated with the recharge period.

If the Recharge Period Window is not visible, display it by clicking "Show" and then selecting "Recharge Period Window". If the window is in the reduced size, click "Expand".

In the Recharge Period Window:

• To delete a single recharge period, click (select) the row containing the recharge period to be deleted.
• To delete recharge periods A through B (inclusive), first click on row A. If necessary, scroll until row B is visible. Then hold down the Shift key and click on row B.
• Click "Delete". The "Confirm" dialog box is displayed.
• Click "Yes" to confirm the deletion.
