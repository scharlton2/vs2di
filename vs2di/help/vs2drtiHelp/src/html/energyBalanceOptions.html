Energy Balance Options

Select any combination of the following energy balance components to be written to the auxiliary output file "balance.out". For each selected component, 3 entries are written: total for simulation, total for time step, and rate for time step.

Specified head, Advection In: Advective energy transport into domain across specified pressure and total head boundaries

Specified head, Advection Out: Advective energy transport out of domain across specified pressure and total head boundaries and seepage faces

Specified Flux, Advection In: Advective energy transport into domain across specified fluid flux boundaries

Specified Flux, Advection Out: Advective energy transport out of domain across specified fluid flux boundaries

Conduction, In: Dispersive and conductive energy transport into domain across all boundaries

Conduction, Out: Dispersive and conductive energy transport out of domain cross all boundaries

Total Energy, In: Total energy into domain by transport and reactions

Total Energy, Out: Total energy out of domain by transport and reactions

Transpiration: Energy transport out of domain by transpiration (plant root extraction)

Change in Energy Storage: Change in energy stored in domain

Energy Balance: Total energy in - total energy out - change in energy storage
