Specifying Observation Points

Note: This step is optional.

Observation points denote locations where simulated total head, pressure head, moisture content, saturation, and concentration (if transport is simulated) are written to file (obsPoints.out) at specified output times.

Select "Observation Points" from the "Active Data" Selector (see ).

You may then:
 •  Draw observation points
 •  Move observation points
 •  Delete observation points
