Tutorial

This tutorial involves simulation of infiltration of warm water and a sodium-chloride solution from land surface through the unsaturated zone. To begin the tutorial, click the "File" menu and select "Open". When the dialog box appears navigate to the folder where the examples files are installed. Go down to the "tutorial" folder and select the file "tutorial.vs2". After clicking the Open button, the domain outline of the example problem will appear. The region to be simulated is approximately 6.5 m in length and 4 m in depth.
