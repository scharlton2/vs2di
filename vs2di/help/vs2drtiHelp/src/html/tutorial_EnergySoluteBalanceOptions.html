Tutorial: Energy Balance Options

In the energy/solute balance panel, balance components for energy and solutes are selected to be written to auxiliary model output file "balance.out". For this example 4 selections are made: Total Energy/Solute Movement In and Out of the Domain, Change in Energy/Solute Storage in the Domain, and Energy/Solute Balance.

After completion of the simulation, "balance.out" will list 3 values for each of these selections (Total for Simulation, Total for Time Step, and Rate for Time Step) for every time step in a format that is suitable for input to standard spreadsheets.

Filling in the energy/solute balance panel completes entries of "Model Options". You should now click the "OK" button. The next step in the tutorial is to view the textural class data.
