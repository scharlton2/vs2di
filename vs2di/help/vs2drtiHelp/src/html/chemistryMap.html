Drawing the Chemistry Map

The chemistry map shows the spatial distribution of chemistry classes (chemical properties). The chemistry map is composed of "chemistry zones" each having one or more uniform properties. Each zone is represented by a polygon (see ).

To draw the chemistry map, select "Chemistry Map" from the "Active Data" Selector (see ).

You may then:
 •  Draw a chemistry zone
 •  Reshape a chemistry zone
 •  Move chemistry zones
 •  Insert a vertex on chemistry zone
 •  Bring chemistry zones to front
 •  Send chemistry zones to back
 •  Delete chemistry zones

Buttons on the left panel allow display of coverages of each of the seven chemical properties. Clicking on one of those buttons will show which chemistry classes are used to define that property.

Notes:

• Chemistry zones may overlap one another. Definitions of property values in those portions of chemistry zones that are covered by other chemistry zones are determined on the basis of options selected when you specify chemistry classes.
• Chemistry zones may extend outside the domain. (They will be "trimmed" by the program.)
• All portions of the domain must be covered by a chemistry zone
