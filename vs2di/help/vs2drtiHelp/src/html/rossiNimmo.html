Rossi-Nimmo Model

The Rossi-Nimmo Junction Model is designed to represent water content and relative hydraulic conductivity over the entire range of saturation. The model is particularly well suited for simulations involving very dry soils. Water content and relative conductivity are determined according to equations 4 through 8 in Rossi and Nimmo (1994). Because of the complexity of those equations, they are not included here. The interested reader should consult Rossi and Nimmo (1994) before selecting this option.

Parameters are:
psi0
psiD
lambda


Reference:
Rossi, C., and Nimmo, J.R., 1994, Modeling of soil water retention
from saturation to oven dryness. Water Resourse Research, v. 30,
no. 3, p. 701-708. http://wwwrcamnl.wr.usgs.gov/uzf/abs_pubs/pubs.html
