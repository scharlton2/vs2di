Showing or Hiding Boundary Condition Labels

Before you begin, make sure that "Boundary Condition" is selected in the "Active Data" Selector (see ).

To show all boundary condition labels, click the button.

To hide all boundary condition labels, click the button.

To show or hide selected boundary condition labels, place the cursor on a boundary segment, hold down the "Alt" key and click the left mouse button. This will alternately show and hide the boundary condition label for that boundary segment.
