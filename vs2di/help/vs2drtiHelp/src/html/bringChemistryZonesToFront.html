Bringing Chemical Zones To Front

Chemical zones that are brought to front may partially or entirely cover other chemical zones.

Before you begin, make sure that "Chemistry Map" is selected in the "Active Data" Selector (see ).

1. Click the  button.

2. If one or more chemical zones are currently "selected" (indicated by small black squares on their vertices), deselect them by clicking on any white space.

3. Select the chemical zone to be brought to front by clicking on it. To select multiple zones, hold down the "Ctrl" key and successively click on each zone.

4. Click the "Edit" menu and select "Bring to Front".
