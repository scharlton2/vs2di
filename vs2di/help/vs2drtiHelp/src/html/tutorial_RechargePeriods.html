Tutorial: Recharge Periods

To view the recharge periods window, select "Recharge Period Window" from the "Show" menu. The window will also be displayed if "Boundary Conditions" is selected from the "Active Data" Selector (see ).

There are 2 recharge periods for the example simulation, the first lasting 0.1 days (8,640 seconds) and the second lasting 10 days (864,000 seconds). The initial time step size is 0.0001 seconds. The time step size will be increased for each successive step by a factor of 1.2 until a maximum step size (100 seconds for recharge period 1, and 2,000 seconds for recharge period 2) is reached. If convergence of the solver is not attained, the time step size will be reduced by a factor of 0.1. The maximum allowed change in head is 10 m. Steady state will only be assumed when there is no change in computed heads between adjacent time steps. If the applied infiltration rate exceeds the ability of the soil to imbibe water, then ponding will occur in the simulation with the elevation of ponded water being equal to that of land surface.
