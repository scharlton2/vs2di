Moving Source/Sink Point

Before you begin, make sure that "Source/Sink Points" is selected in the "Active Data" Selector (see ).

1. Click the  button.

2. Move the cursor to the source/sink point.

Now, you may either:

3a. Press and hold the left mouse button. The source/sink point will become highlighted.

4a. With the button still pressed, move (drag) the cursor to the desired new location.

5a. Release the mouse button and the source/sink point will be moved to the new location.

or:

3b. Right click on the source/sink point. A popup menu appears. Select "Properties".

4b. A dialog box will appear, showing the current x-z coordinates of the source/sink point.

5b. Enter the new x-z coordinates and click OK.
