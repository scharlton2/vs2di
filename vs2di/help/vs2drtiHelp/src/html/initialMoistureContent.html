Specifying Initial Moisture Content

Begin by selecting "Initial Moisture Content" from the "Active Data" Selector (see ). If this option is not available, you will need to change the type of initial hydraulic condition.

The distribution of initial moisture content is specified by drawing contours of moisture content. Please see the section on "Contour Drawing and Interpolation" for instructions on how to draw contours.

Note: The initial moisture content at any point in the domain must be smaller than or equal to the porosity, and greater than or equal to the residual moisture content of the textural class at that point.
