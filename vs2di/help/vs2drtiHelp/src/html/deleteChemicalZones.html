Deleting Chemical Zones

Before you begin, make sure that "Chemical Map" is selected in the "Active Data" Selector (see ).

1. Click the  button.

2. If one or more chemical zones are currently "selected" (indicated by small black squares on their vertices), deselect them by clicking on any white space.

3. Select the chemical zone to be deleted by clicking on it. To select multiple zones, hold down the "Ctrl" key and successively click on each zone. To select all zones, click the "Edit" menu and select "Select All".

4. Click the "Edit" menu and select "Delete", or press the "Del" key.
