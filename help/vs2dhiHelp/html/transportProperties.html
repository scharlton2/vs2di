Transport Properties

• Long. disp. (alpha-L): [L] Longitudinal dispersivity.
• Trans. disp. (alpha-T): [L} Transverse dispersivity.
• Heat capacity (dry) (Cs): Heat capacity of dry solids, [Q/L3 oC], where Q is energy or ML2T-2. For SI units, heat capacities are in J/m3 oC.
• Kt(RMC): Thermal conductivity of water-sediment at residual moisture content, [Q/LToC]. For SI units thermal conductivities are in W/moC.
• Kt(saturated or Por): Thermal conductivity of water-sediment at full saturation, [Q/LToC].
• Heat capacity(water) (Cw): Heat capacity of water, which is the product of density times specific heat of water, [Q/L3 oC].
