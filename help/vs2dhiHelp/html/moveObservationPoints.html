Moving Observation Points

Before you begin, make sure that "Observation Points" is selected in the "Active Data" Selector (see ).

1. Click the  button.

2. Move the cursor to the observation point.

Now, you may either:

3a. Press and hold the left mouse button. The observation point will become highlighted.

4a. With the button still pressed, move (drag) the cursor to the desired new location.

5a. Release the mouse button and the observation point will be moved to the new location.

or:

3b. Right click on the observation point. A dialog box will appear, showing the current x-z coordinates of the observation point.

4b. Enter the new x-z coordinates and click OK.
