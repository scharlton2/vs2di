Setting the Evapotranspiration Period Length

If the Evapotranspiration Window is not visible, display it by clicking "Show" and then selecting "Evapotranspiration Window".

In the Evapotranspiration Window:

1. Click "Modify". The "Evapotranspiration Period Length" dialog box is displayed.

2. Enter the evapotranspiration period length

3. Click "OK".

Note: All evapotranspiration periods have the same length.
