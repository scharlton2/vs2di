Flow Options

Initial hydraulic condition may be specified as

• Equilibrium profile: The initial pressure head equals negative elevation head above a water table. The user may also replace the upper part of the equilibrium profile by a constant minimum pressure head.
• Pressure head: As specified by the user.
• Moisture content: As specified by the user.

Intercell relative hydraulic conductivity may be computed by:

• Arithmetic mean: The preferred method.
• Geometric mean: An alternative to the arithmetic mean.
• Upstream weighting: May be needed when simulating the movement of a sharp wetting front into a dry medium.
