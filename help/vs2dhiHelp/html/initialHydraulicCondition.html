Specifying Initial Hydraulic Condition

Initial Hydraulic condition may be specified as one of the following:

1. Initial equilibrium profile: the pressure head equals the negative elevation head above a water table. The user may also replace the upper part of the equilibrium profile by a constant minimum pressure head. (see ).

2. Initial pressure head: Specified by drawing contour lines of equal pressure head (see ).

3. Initial moisture content: Specified by drawing contour lines of equal moisture content (see ). (This option is not available when the hydraulic characteristic function is given by tabular data.)

Notes:

• When feasible, starting the simulation from an equilibrium state is recommended.
• If initial condition does not represent an equilibrium state, then the simulation will include transient effects arising from the initial non-equilibrium state.
