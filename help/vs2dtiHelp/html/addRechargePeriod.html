Adding One or More Recharge Periods

If the Recharge Period Window is not visible, display it by clicking "Show" and then selecting "Recharge Period Window". If the window is in the reduced size, click "Expand".

In the Recharge Period Window:

1. To add recharge period(s) starting at row n, click (select) row n - 1. The new period(s) will be inserted after the selected row. Skip this step when the table is empty.

2. Click "Add". The "Add Recharge Period" dialog box is displayed.

3. Enter the recharge period parameters.

4. Click "OK".
