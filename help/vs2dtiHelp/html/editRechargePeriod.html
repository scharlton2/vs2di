Editing One or More Recharge Periods

If the Recharge Period Window is not visible, display it by clicking "Show" and then selecting "Recharge Period Window". If the window is in the reduced size, click "Expand".

In the Recharge Period Window:

• To edit a single recharge period, click (select) the row containing the recharge period to be edited.
• To edit recharge periods A through B (inclusive), first click on row A. If necessary, scroll until row B is visible. Then hold down the Shift key and click on row B.
• Click "Edit". The "Edit Recharge Period" dialog box is displayed.
• Change the recharge period parameters.
• Click "OK".
