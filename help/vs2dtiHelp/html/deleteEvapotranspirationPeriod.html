Deleting an Evapotranspiration Period

If the Evapotranspiration Window is not visible, display it by clicking "Show" and then selecting "Evapotranspiration Window".

In the Evapotranspiration Window:

1. Click (select) the row containing the evapotranspiration period to be deleted.

2. Click "Delete". The "Confirm" dialog box is displayed.

3. Click "Yes" to confirm the deletion.
