Contour Drawing and Interpolation

Contour lines are used to specify the initial distribution of pressure head, moisture content, or concentration.

 •  Reshape a contour

 •  Specify the interpolation method

Notes:

• A contour must not intersect itself or other contours.
• Contour values may be shown or hidden by toggling the button.
• An open contour line should not end inside the domain. In other words, both endpoints of the contour line should fall outside the domain.
• The value at a point in the domain is estimated from the contours by using one of the interpolation methods.
